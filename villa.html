Our Mission:

Creating Unforgettable Stays for Our Guests

Bedrooms

The villa features four spacious bedrooms, each with its own en-suite bathroom, along with two separate bathrooms. Additional sleeping accommodations are available, accommodating a maximum of 10 guests, with an optimal capacity of 8.

Kitchen

Inside, the villa boasts a modern, fully-equipped kitchen complete with a coffee maker, microwave, and all the essential appliances. A washer and dryer add convenience to your stay.

Outside Area

Step into our inviting outdoor haven. Enjoy the bar while you relax by the heated pool and jacuzzi and let kids have fun on the swing set, slide, and trampoline. Dine al fresco in complete privacy. Just 2km from beaches, markets, and dining options, our outdoor area offers convenience and serenity for a memorable stay.

Gym & Game Room

Our on-site gym and game room are designed for ultimate enjoyment and convenience. Keep your fitness routine in check in our fully-equipped gym, or indulge in some friendly competition with billiards, darts, and table football in our game room. Fun and fitness, all within arm's reach.
